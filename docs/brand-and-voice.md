# Brand & voice

> **Welcome to (the real).**

## DNA

Diametral is **Minimal · Enduring · Elegant**. A refined, structured aesthetic, deliberately
**away from the typical color codes of tech and consulting** — flat and sharp, with 1px rules,
no shadows and no border-radius, white and whitesmoke surfaces, black ink, and a single signal
red as the only accent. The system is meant to age well: quiet, durable, and confident rather
than trend-driven.

## The symbol

The mark is three elements, each carrying meaning:

- **The circle** — the space of intelligence and complexity.
- **The square** — the structure that brings stability.
- **The line** — Diametral's clear positioning.

Together they state the brand's premise: complexity held inside a stable structure, with a
clear point of view. The flat, square visual language is the symbol made into an interface —
structure first, no decoration for its own sake.

## Positioning

Diametral steps deliberately away from the clichés of tech and consulting: no gradients, no
soft drop-shadows, no rounded "friendly" corners, no neon-on-dark dashboards. It favours the
real — material, structured, restrained. The result should feel closer to fine print and
considered industrial design than to a generic SaaS UI.

## Voice

UI copy follows the same discipline as the visuals.

- **Terse.** Say it once, plainly. Prefer short labels and short sentences over explanation.
- **Structured.** Use **uppercase labels** (`.ds-label` / `.ds-kicker`, tracked at `0.08em`)
  to mark structure — sections, field names, kickers — without raising the volume.
- **No jargon, no hype.** Avoid marketing adjectives, exclamation marks, and buzzwords. State
  facts and outcomes. "Saved." not "Awesome — your changes are saved!"
- **Numbers are first-class.** Figures use tabular numerals so they align in columns; write
  them precisely and consistently.
- **Title voice is reserved.** The light Ufficio (Light 300) titles carry headings and hero
  figures; body copy stays in quiet Geist. Don't mix the two voices arbitrarily.

## Photography

Photography direction is **natural macro texture** — organic and material, never tech cliché.
Two registers anchor the palette:

- **Warm walnut wood** — close, tactile grain; the warm side of the brand.
- **Cool arctic ice / water** — crystalline, cold, clear; the cool side.

Shoot macro and let the material fill the frame: real surfaces, real light, no renders, no
device mockups, no abstract gradients, no stock-tech imagery (servers, circuit boards, glowing
lines). The image should feel like something you could touch.
