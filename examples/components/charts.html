Components

Charts

A small set of pure-SVG charts — line, area and donut — with no chart library. Each shares the flat .ds-chart wrapper, faint .ds-chart__grid gridlines, .ds-chart__axis labels and a wrap-friendly .ds-chart-legend. Series colors come from a tiny palette of --ds-* tokens. Flat, sharp, no radius, no shadow.

Line

A .ds-linechart draws one __line polyline per series across a shared y-range, over optional gridlines and x-axis labels, with a dot on every point.

Area

A .ds-areachart mirrors the line chart but fills a semi-transparent __area band from each line down to the baseline. The fill uses the series color via fill-opacity.

Single series · filled band

Donut

A .ds-donut draws each segment as a __segment circle stroke with a stroke-dasharray arc, a faint __track behind, an optional big __center number, and a .ds-chart-legend of label + value.
