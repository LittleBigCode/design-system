Banner

A full-width, fixed-identity notice. It stays pale yellow with dark ink in every theme, making it the right home for a persistent, machine-level message. Emphasize key words with <b>.

Default

A single ds-banner spans the width of its container; add the hidden attribute to dismiss it.
